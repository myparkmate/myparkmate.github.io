Services

This is what we do.

Create a profile

Set up your personal profile on MyParkMate in order to gain access to the marketplace and payment system.

Post a spot

List the address of your spot, choose the duration of the spot's availabity, and set your pricing. You can add relevant tags to your spot to increase visibilty.

Search for a spot

Whether your looking for an overnight spot or a spot for 30min, search the MyParkMate data base by location and select a suitable spot. Once you have found your spot, pay and park!
